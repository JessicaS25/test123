Montessori Inspired Children's furniture

What is Montessori and why is it useful?

Montessori is an educational method that focusses on child led and hands-on learning.

The use of the bespoke kitchen and bathroom unit encourages the completion of daily tasks such as brushing teeth and preparing food.

Toddler sized tables and chairs is a perfect way of making the leap from high chair to "adult chair". The dimensions mean that table manners and cutlery skills can be mastered without fear of falling or stretching across adult furniture.
